About Us

Eldevan Services is an investigation and security consultancy company that provides all investigative and security services in Kenya since 2016 with professionalism and timely. We are customer friendly and work towards solving our customer needs.
Just as our slogan denotes "Exposing the hidden to better the society". We are committed to bring out that which is hidden to light and ensure your security and investigation problems are solved.
Our Principal Activities include :

  • Security

  • Safety

  • Risk Consultancy

HISTORY AND PHILOSOPHY

Eldevan services is an outstanding consultancy company that began as a security and safety training service provider in the year 2016. Since then the company has undertaken a number of trainings to individuals, companies and institutions on matters of safety and security Risks; emphasizing on proper methods of identifying and preventing the risk from actual human harm and property damage.
Today eldevan Services has taken a role in providing professional services in regards to control room operations, security risks, Front office management (Security Risk receptionists), Security Audits, safety and related risks analyses.
As Eldevan services our execution philosophy is:

  • Create detail schedule and resource plan to meet client’s objectives.
  • To communicate early with all our clients in relations to our services delivery.
  • To supervise closely to ensure professionalism amongst all our employees.

We have always taken pride in our service delivery and our clients can always be assured that only the most experienced and qualified are serving them, all the time.
